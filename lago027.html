About Lakefront Running

I believe the reason for a coach is to pass on knowledge. My goal is to see other runners prosper because of the hard work they put in by having someone believe in them. When you have someone to help feed your goals and help turn them into a reality it is magical. Lakefront Running aims to provide coaching and custom training plans to competitive runners or runners looking to be more competitive of all ages. Lakefront Running focuses on the process of doing a little bit each day to grow over time instead of focusing on a specific outcome.

About the Coach

Hello, I’m Kasey Levinsohn—a dedicated runner with a passion for track and cross country. Through my competitive journey in high school, I’ve developed a deep appreciation for the transformative power of running. One principle that guides my coaching philosophy is the pursuit of consistency—a cornerstone in achieving lasting success and personal fulfillment in running. I’m committed to helping athletes harness their potential and achieve their goals through structured training and a love for the sport.

Age Groups

Middle School

For Middle School, you will focus on building a consistent routine while incorporating some bodyweight workouts.

High School

For High School, it’s about managing volume and intensity while also working on the process and the constant pursuit of consistency.

Post-High School

For Post-High School, it’s about maximizing each workout and run by using productive recovery methods, threshold runs, higher volume, more miles, and more.
